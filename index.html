How we met

Wow, how time flies! Has it really been 6-8 years since we graduated from Houghton College, where we all met? That is crazy, huh! Who would have guessed we'd still be doing this all these years later!

Our family grows

Throughout college we got closer and made more friends, like Alex, who has since been in charge of all relevant social media for the group!

It's hard to think what we would do if there wasn't an event page set up for these reunions. Honestly, we'd all be in the dark without it!!

We graduated!

After only four to six years of learning together, and especially hating geology, we ordered our caps and gowns! Time to hit the real world, guys!!

Time to celebrate

And what better way than with a reunion every single year! But uh oh, isn't it hard to organize somewhere between 17 and 22 people in the months from March until August, in preperation for the reunion that might be in either August, September, or both?

Nope! Not with the awesome, and easy to navigate event page that is set up for these reunions. Phew!

Some things are short but friends are forever.
